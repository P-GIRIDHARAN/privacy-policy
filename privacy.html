Privacy Policy – Sound Booster

Last updated: September 26, 2025

Introduction

Sound Booster is a Chrome extension designed to amplify audio and video playback within your browser. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Sound Booster does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The extension operates locally in your browser and only modifies audio playback on web pages you choose to boost.

Information Usage

No data is transmitted to any server. The extension solely amplifies audio and does not monitor or log your activity.

Permissions

Sound Booster requests minimal permissions necessary for functionality:

  • activeTab: to access the current tab where audio is being boosted.
  • scripting: to inject the audio booster content script into the active tab.
  • Host permission (<all_urls>): to modify audio elements on web pages you choose.

User Control

You can disable, reset, or remove Sound Booster at any time through Chrome’s extensions page. No settings or activity are transmitted externally.

Contact

Certification

By using Sound Booster, you acknowledge that:

  • No personal data is collected or sold.
  • Data is not used for purposes unrelated to audio boosting.
  • Data is not used for creditworthiness or lending purposes.
